
= Kampf-System


D&D 5e + BG3 Adaptationen mit Surfaces, Gravity, Cover, Foundry VTT Integration, Encounter-Generierung.


== Kern-Mechanik: D&D 5e + BG3



=== Initiative & Turn Order


----
Combat starts:
  All combatants roll Initiative (1d20 + DEX Modifier)
  Highest to Lowest determines order
  System auto-orders + shows on initiative tracker

Each Turn has:
  1 Action (Attack, Cast Spell, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Help)
  1 Bonus Action (if class feature allows)
  Movement (30ft base, adjusted by armor/condition)
  1 Reaction (opportunity attack, shield spell, etc.)
  Interact with 1 object (draw weapon, drink potion, etc.)
----


=== Attack Roll


----
Attack Roll = 1d20 + Attack Bonus
  Attack Bonus = Ability Modifier + Proficiency (if proficient)

vs. Target AC

Hit: Roll Damage Dice + Modifiers
Miss: No damage

Nat 20: Critical Hit (roll damage twice)
Nat 1: Always Miss (regardless of modifiers)
----


=== Spell Casting


_BG3 Spell Slot System_:

----
Spell Slots (not mana):
  Per spell LEVEL (1-9)
  current_slots / max_slots always visible

Casting a spell:
  Costs 1 Spell Slot of appropriate level (or higher with upcasting)

Cantrips (0-level):
  Unlimited uses
  Skalieren with Character Level (not Class Level)
  Can use Bonus Action or Action

Upcasting:
  Cast 3rd-level spell in 4th-level slot
  Effekt wird stärker (if scalable)
  Cost: 1 Slot of higher level
----

'''


== Surfaces & Environmental Hazards (BG3-inspired)



=== Surface Types



[cols="1,1,1,1",options="header"]
|===
| Surface | Created By | Effect | Duration

| Fire | Fireball, torch | 1d4 damage per turn standing | Until extinguished
| Ice | Frost spells, water frozen | Slippery (DEX DC 10 to not fall) | Until melted
| Web | Spider web, Web spell | Restrained, difficult terrain | Until burned
| Poison | Gas cloud, green dragon | 1d6 poison damage per turn | Until dispersed
| Blood | Defeated creature | Slippery (DC 10) | Stays until cleaned
| Grease | Grease spell, oil | Slippery (DC 10) | 1 hour or burned
| Tar | Trap, magical effect | Difficult terrain, stuck (DC 12 STR) | Until removed
|===



=== Surface Interaction


----
Charakter steht auf Fire:
  Start of Turn: Take damage ODER move away
  Can use Action to stop drop and roll (DC 10 DEX)

Charakter nächst zu Web:
  Can walk durch (restrained while inside)
  Can burn it (fire damage clears it)

Encounter Designer puts surfaces STRATEGICALLY:
  Fire between party and enemies (tactical choice: avoid or cross?)
  Ice on bridge (high stakes crossing)
  Poison cloud in cave (use ventilation?)
----

'''


== Gravity & Verticality



=== Falling Damage


----
3d6 damage per 10 feet fallen (minimum 1d6)

Beispiele:
  Fall 10 feet: 3d6 damage
  Fall 20 feet: 6d6 damage
  Fall 100 feet: 30d6 damage (instant death?)

Damaging Fall > Max HP:
  Character dies

Dex Save to grab ledge (DC = 10 + distance/10):
  Success: Grab ledge, take no damage, hang
  Failure: Fall
----


=== Flying Combat


----
Character with flight (winged, levitate spell):
  Can move in 3D space (up/down/diagonal)
  Ranged attacks can target flying enemies
  Flying enemies can swoop + attack + swoop away

High ground advantage:
  +2 to attack rolls
  Enemy disadvantage if trying to reach you

Flying + Spell Casting:
  Must maintain concentration to stay flying
  If hit: CON Save to maintain flight
----

'''


== Cover & Concealment



=== Cover Levels



[cols="1,1,1,1",options="header"]
|===
| Cover | AC Bonus | DEX Save Bonus | Example

| Half Cover | +2 | +2 | Standing behind pillar
| 3/4 Cover | +5 | +5 | Behind wall with gaps
| Full Cover | Immunity | Immunity | Completely hidden
| Soft Cover | +1 | +1 | Enemies bodies (not protective)
|===



=== Example


----
Rogue hides behind pillar (half cover, +2 AC)
Archer shoots from 60 feet away (no cover)

Archer's attack: 1d20 + 6 (normal) = 12
vs. Rogue's AC: 16 (base) + 2 (cover) = 18

Miss! Rogue is protected
----

'''

== Foundry VTT Integration


System auto-syncs with Foundry Virtual Tabletop:


=== Automated Pushes to Foundry


----
Combat Initialization:
  → Party loads Foundry Scene
  → System reads encounter file
  → Enemy tokens appear on map
  → Initiative rolled, combatants ordered

During Combat:
  → PC's turn: Foundry highlights this PC
  → PC declares action: System log synced
  → Damage rolled: HP bars in Foundry update
  → Death: Token grayed out
  → Loot: Post-combat sync to loot table

After Combat:
  → Combat ends in system
  → Loot automatically generated
  → Encounter-log created
  → Foundry scene cleared
----


=== GM Tools in Foundry


----
Encounter Designer:
  - Drag enemies from library
  - Set positions, hp override, treasure
  - Difficulty rating shown (Easy/Medium/Hard/Deadly)
  - Suggest adjustments if too easy/hard

Combat Tracker:
  - Initiative order visible
  - Current turn highlighted
  - HP bars synced
  - Conditions visible (icons)

Token Tools:
  - Drag token to new position (resolves movement cost)
  - Right-click for actions (attack, spell)
  - Damage taken syncs to system
  - Status effects shown (frozen icon, burning aura, etc.)
----

'''


== Encounter-Generierung



=== Difficulty Rating


----
Encounter XP = Sum of (Creature CR × Creature Count)

Party Level 5 (4 characters):
  Easy: < 400 XP
  Medium: 400-800 XP
  Hard: 800-1600 XP
  Deadly: > 1600 XP

System suggests:
  "Add 1 more bandit to make Medium"
  "Remove bugbear chief for easier encounter"
----


=== Random Encounter Tables


----
encounter_tables (Welt)
├── region_id (FK)
├── terrain_type (grassland, forest, etc.)
├── difficulty_range (1-5)
├── creatures[] (jsonb)
│   ├── {creature_id, cr, count_range, frequency}
├── treasure_multiplier (×0.5 for weak, ×2.0 for boss)
└── special_conditions (burn nearby, cave ceiling, etc.)
----


=== Encounter Scaling


----
Party is:
  2 characters instead of 4? → Reduce encounter count by -2
  Level 5 instead of 3? → Increase difficulty tier
  Weakened (low HP, no spells)? → Use easier table
----

'''


== Combat Sequence (Typical)


----
[PREPARATION]
GM: "You're ambushed by 3 bandits!"
System: Generates encounter from table
  - 3 Bandits (CR 1/8 each)
  - 45 GP total
  - 1 dagger (Uncommon Q1)
Foundry scene loads

[INITIALIZATION]
Initiative rolled:
  Player A: 18
  Bandit 1: 14
  Player B: 12
  Bandit 2: 11
  Bandit 3: 9

[ROUND 1]
Player A's Turn: "I cast Fireball!"
  → Enemies in range make DEX saves
  → System rolls damage (4d6 = 14 total)
  → Enemies take damage: Bandit 1 (14 dmg, now 2 hp remaining)

Bandit 1's Turn: "Flanks Player B, attacks!"
  → 1d20 + 4 vs AC 14
  → Roll: 16 (hit!)
  → 1d6 + 2 damage = 5 dmg
  → Player B now 27/32 HP

[COMBAT CONTINUES]
... multiple rounds ...

[VICTORY]
Bandits defeated!
Loot generated:
  - 45 GP divided (11 each, 2 remainder)
  - Dagger (can be taken by one PC)
  - Bodies left for scavenging

[LOOT PHASE]
System: "Distribute loot"
GM assigns to inventory
Gold added to party_gold
Experience calculated:
  450 XP total (3 bandits × 150 base)
  112 XP per character
  Each PC gains level progress

[AFTERMATH]
System logs combat:
  - Damage taken by each PC
  - Resources used (spells, items)
  - Decisions made
  - Trait-relevant moments noted
Session continues with travel/rest
----

'''


== Special Combat Rules



=== Bonus Action Limitations


----
Can only use ONE bonus action per turn, even if multiple exist:
  Priest has: Bonus Action Healing + Bonus Action Spiritual Weapon
  Turn: Can use ONE

Lagen spells (Action + bonus action):
  If cast Bonus Action spell → can only cast 0-level (cantrip) as action
  CANNOT cast two leveled spells in same turn
----


=== Opportunity Attacks


----
Enemy leaves threatened area:
  Trigger opportunity attack (if reaction available)

Can't trigger if:
  - Enemy used Disengage action
  - Enemy teleported away
  - Enemy flew straight up (if no flying)

1 opportunity attack max per turn (from this enemy source)
----


=== Concentration


----
Spell requires concentration:
  While concentrating on Spell A, can't cast Spell B (if B also concentration)

Takes damage:
  CON Save DC 10 or (damage/2, whichever higher)
  Fail: Concentration breaks
  Success: Maintain spell

Only one concentration active at a time
----

== DB-Struktur


----
creatures (Welt)
├── id (PK)
├── name
├── creature_type (humanoid|beast|undead|etc)
├── ac
├── hp
├── speed_ft
├── ability_scores (STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA)
├── skills (proficiency + bonus)
├── actions[] (jsonb: [{name, action_type, damage, dc}])
├── reactions[] (jsonb)
├── special_abilities[] (jsonb)
├── resistances (damage types)
├── immunities (damage types)
├── senses (darkvision range, passive perception)
├── languages
├── challenge_rating (cr)
└── treasure_table (loot reference)

combats (Kampagne)
├── id (PK)
├── session_id (FK)
├── scene_id (FK)
├── start_time_harptos
├── end_time_harptos
├── encounter_difficulty (easy|medium|hard|deadly)
├── participants[] (jsonb: {character_id, npc_id, side: party|enemy})
├── combat_round (current round)
├── xp_total
├── treasure_generated (jsonb)
└── resolution (victory|defeat|fled|custom)

combat_damage_log (Kampagne-Log)
├── combat_id (FK)
├── round_number
├── attacker_id (character|npc)
├── target_id (character|npc)
├── damage_amount
├── damage_type
├── hit_or_miss
└── condition_applied (if any)

encounter_templates (Welt)
├── id (PK)
├── region_id (FK)
├── terrain_type
├── difficulty
├── creatures[] (jsonb: [{creature_id, count_range}])
├── surfaces (jsonb)
├── special_conditions (jsonb)
└── reward_multiplier
----
