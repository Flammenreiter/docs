
= Organisations-System


Organisations-Typen mit Rang-Systemen, Membership-Anforderungen, Exclusion-Regeln, automatische Reputation-Vererbung.


== Organisations-Typen



[cols="1,1,1,1",options="header"]
|===
| Typ | Beispiel | Struktur | Membership

| Akademie | Mage's Guild, University | Hierarchisch, Level-based | Earned through training
| Gilde | Thieves Guild, Blacksmith Guild | Rank-based, meritocratic | Sponsor-required
| Militär | Royal Army, City Guard | Military hierarchy | Application + background check

| Informal | Merchant caravan, gang | Loose, skill-based | Reputation-based

== Membership-System



=== Beitrittsprozess


----
Character wants to join "Thieves Guild":

Step 1: Sponsor finden
  - Muss jemand kennen der "Thieves Guild" member ist
  - Oder Reputation 40+ mit Guild
  - Sponsor-NPC führt ein Interview

Step 2: Anforderungen überprüfen
  System prüft:
  ├─ Trait-Requirements (Gierig+ maybe, Ehrlich NOT)
  ├─ Level Minimum (usually 1+)
  ├─ Exclusion-Rules (not member of rival org)
  ├─ Criminal record (might be okay? depends)

Step 3: Initiation-Quest
  GM creates custom quest based on org type:
  - Akademie: "Write thesis on your school"
  - Gilde: "Craft item for guild show"
  - Militär: "Survive training montage"
  - Kult: "Prove faith through trial"

Step 4: Acceptance
  Once completed:
  ├─ Character gains Rank 0 (lowest)
  ├─ Reputation auto-set to +10 with org
  ├─ Mechanical bonuses activate
  ├─ Access to org resources
----


=== In-Org-Advancement (Rank-Up)


----
To advance rank within org:

Character-Level Requirements:
  ├─ Reach X XP / Level
  └─ Complete advancement quest

Social Requirements:
  ├─ Disposition +30+ with leadership NPC
  └─ No disciplinary incidents

Performance Requirements:
  ├─ Complete X tasks for org
  └─ Demonstrate mastery (skill checks)

Advancement Quest:
  ├─ Sponsor NPC proposes challenge
  ├─ Character completes
  ├─ Org leaders formally vote
  └─ Ceremony (roleplay moment)
----

'''


== Exclusion-Regeln (Org-Level)



=== Hard Exclusion (Absolute)


----
Character cannot be member of BOTH simultaneously:

Example:
  - Royal Army (Kingdom Military) ↔ Thieves Guild
  - Paladins' Order ↔ Dark Cult
  - Merchant's Guild ↔ Thieves Guild (maybe)

Rules:
  - Joining Organization A auto-removes from Organization B
  - If already member: Must quit before joining new
  - Some orgs don't allow dual-membership (Akademie often strict)
----


=== Soft Exclusion (Penalty)


----
Can be member of both, but penalties apply:

Example:
  - Holy Priesthood + Arcane Mages' Guild
  - Allowed but: -2 on both orgs' checks during conflict

Represents:
  - Ideological conflict
  - Divided loyalty (both orgs know you're in both)
  - Must declare allegiance during org conflict
----


=== Temporal Exclusion (Time-based)


----
Can't rejoin same org for X time after leaving:

Example:
  - Leave Army (dishonorably): 1 year ban
  - Leave Guild (resigned): 6 months
  - Leave Akademie (expelled): 2 years

Represents:
  - Reputation damage
  - Trust broken
  - Must prove worthiness to re-enter
----

'''


== Reputation Auto-Inheritance


Character has individual Reputation with org. But Party also has Org Reputation.


=== Calculation


----
Character Individual Reputation:
  - What THIS character has earned through actions
  - Ranges -100 to +100 per org

Party Org Reputation:
  - What GROUP has earned (average)
  - Ranges -100 to +100 per org

When interacting with Org:
  Effective Reputation = 60% (individual) + 40% (party)

Example:
  Character: +60 with Royal Army
  Party: +30 with Royal Army (others don't have high rep)
  Effective: 0.6(60) + 0.4(30) = 36 + 12 = +48
----


=== Contagion


High party reputation helps, but also can hurt:

----
If Party has +80 reputation with Thieves Guild:
  All NEW members benefit (+20 automatic bonus on first impression)
  But if one character commits crime:
    Whole party reputation takes hit (-30 for major crime)
    "You're part of that guild that betrayed us"
----

'''


== Org-Specific Mechanics



=== Akademie (Learning)


----
Members can:
  - Use library (free research)
  - Buy potions cheaply (-30%)
  - Request NPC casting (50+ GP)
  - Learn new spells (from library)
  - Attend lectures (roleplay, minor XP)

Higher Ranks:
  - Can teach others (passive income)
  - Can request scribing services
  - Can access restricted sections (dangerous knowledge)
  - Can propose research projects
----


=== Gilde (Crafting)


----
Members can:
  - Sell items in guild shop
  - Buy materials cheaply (-20%)
  - Commission crafting (guild crafters do work for you)
  - Share knowledge (learn new recipes)
  - Participate in guild competitions (prizes)

Higher Ranks:
  - Can lead guild projects
  - Set local prices/standards
  - Can reject members (enforcement)
  - Earn passive income from guild activities
----


=== Militär (Combat)


----
Members can:
  - Use military resources
  - Request support (soldiers, equipment)
  - Get paid for patrols/quests
  - Immunity from city guards (up to rank 2)
  - Stable housing

Higher Ranks:
  - Can command soldiers
  - Access to war strategy info
  - Can requisition equipment
  - Make strategic decisions
  - Lead military operations
----


=== Kult (Powers)


----
Members gain:
  - Ritual access (once per week)
  - Divine/arcane connection bonuses
  - Sanctuary in cult sites
  - Dark knowledge (secrets)
  - Passive abilities (ex: See in Darkness)

Higher Ranks:
  - Can perform rituals FOR others (cost)
  - Can communicate with deity/patron
  - Can induct new members
  - Access to forbidden spells
  - Special items

Costs:
  - Must attend religious services (monthly minimum)
  - Cannot break oath (violation = permanent damage)
  - Conflicting orgs automatically excluded
----

'''


== Org-Related Quests



=== Org Quests (Repeatable)


----
Each org offers repeatable quests:

Akademie Quest:
  "Research component: Fetch moonstone from mountain cave"
  Reward: 50 GP + org XP

Gilde Quest:
  "Deliver order to neighboring town"
  Reward: 75 GP + org XP

Militär Quest:
  "Patrol border for bandits"
  Reward: 100 GP + org XP

Kult Quest:
  "Retrieve ritual component (rare)"
  Reward: 50 GP + mysterious power + org XP
----


=== Org-Rank-Up Quests


----
To reach Rank 2, might get quest:
  "Prove your worth: Defeat apprentice mage in duel"
  OR
  "Create masterwork item for showcase"
  OR
  "Lead soldiers in battle"
  OR
  "Survive ritual of revelation"

Custom-generated per org/rank
----

'''

== DB-Struktur


----
organizations (Welt)
├── id (PK)
├── name
├── type (enum: academy|guild|military|noble|cult|informal)
├── description
├── leader_npc_id (FK)
├── headquarters_location_id (FK)
├── rank_system[] (jsonb: [{rank_number, title, requirements, bonuses}])
├── exclusion_rules[] (jsonb: [{org_id, exclusion_type}])
├── membership_requirements (jsonb: {min_level, trait_requirements[]})
└── quests[] (jsonb: [quest_id])

character_organization_membership (Kampagne)
├── character_id (FK)
├── organization_id (FK)
├── current_rank (0-max)
├── rank_progress_xp (towards next rank)
├── joined_date_harptos
├── individual_reputation (-100 to +100)
├── rank_quests_completed[] (jsonb)
└── status (active|suspended|expelled)

faction_relationships (Kampagne)
├── party_group_id (FK)
├── organization_id (FK)
├── party_reputation (-100 to +100)
├── last_interaction_harptos
└── interaction_history (jsonb)

organization_ranks (Welt)
├── id (PK)
├── organization_id (FK)
├── rank_number (0-N)
├── title
├── description
├── xp_requirement
├── mechanical_bonuses[] (jsonb)
├── privileges[] (jsonb)
└── responsibilities[] (jsonb: what must rank do)
----
