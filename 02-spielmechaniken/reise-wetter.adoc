
= Reise-System & Wetter


Reise-Routen, tägliche Pacing, Begegnungen nach Typ-Verteilung, Proviant, monatliche Wetter-Kalibration.


== Reise-Routen



=== Route-Struktur


Jede Route verbindet zwei Settlement-Nodes:

----
Route Properties:
├── origin_settlement_id
├── destination_settlement_id
├── distance_miles
├── terrain_type (grassland|mountain|forest|desert|water|mixed)
├── difficulty (easy|moderate|hard|deadly)
├── hazards[] (brigands, monsters, weather, etc.)
├── safe_stops[] (waypoints for resting)
└── historical_events (lore)
----


=== Terrain-Types und ihre Effekte



[cols="1,1,1,1",options="header"]
|===
| Terrain | Speed | Weather | Hazards

| Grassland | Normal | Moderate | Brigands, wild horses
| Mountain | -25% | Extreme | Avalanche, cold, altitude
| Forest | -10% | Moderate | Bandits, predators, getting lost
| Desert | -30% | Extreme heat | Dehydration, sandstorm, nomads
| Water | Variable | Extreme | Drowning, pirates, krakens
| Mixed | -15% | Variable | All hazards possible
|===


'''


== Pacing-Optionen (täglich)


Spieler wählen tägliche Pace:


=== Careful Pace (Exploration Speed)


----
Speed: ×0.5 (Half-day progress)
Stealth: Advantage (+2)
Perception: +2 to spot ambush
Provision Cost: ×1 (normal)
Rest: Can have full camp rituals
Encounters: ×1 (single encounter possible)
Risk: Low
----

_Wann_: Wenn Gruppe erwartet Ärger oder erkundet


=== Normal Pace


----
Speed: ×1.0 (Full-day progress)
Stealth: Normal
Perception: Normal
Provision Cost: ×1 (normal)
Rest: Standard
Encounters: ×1 (standard chance)
Risk: Medium
----

_Wann_: Default Option


=== Forced March (Fleeing/Urgent)


----
Speed: ×1.5 (Extra progress)
Stealth: Disadvantage (-2)
Perception: -2 to spot hazards
Provision Cost: ×1.5 (extra food/water needed)
Rest: Shortened rests only
Encounters: ×2 (doubles encounter chance)
Risk: High
Constitution Save DC 10 + 1/additional day
  Fail: 1 Exhaustion per day
----

_Wann_: Fleeing dragon, urgent deadline

'''


== Tägliche Encounters


Nach jeder Travel-Phase (1 day), würfele Encounter-Chance:


=== Encounter-Chance


----
Base Chance: 30%
+ Terrain Modifier: +10-30% (desert +30%, grassland +10%)
+ Pacing Modifier: Careful -10%, Normal 0, Forced +20%
+ Recent Combat: +30% if fought yesterday

Roll: If >threshold → Encounter happens
----


=== Encounter-Typ Verteilung


----
Combat (~30%):     Bandits, Monster, feindliche Patrouille
Social (~25%):     Reisende Händler, verlorener NPC, Flüchtlinge
Discovery (~15%):  Ruine, Schatz-Anhaltspunkt, Höhle, Wegschrein
Environmental (~15%): Brücke kaputt, Erdrutsch, Flussüberquerung
Omen/Lore (~10%):  Merkwürdiges Zeichen, Prophezeiung, magische Anomalie
Trap/Ambush (~5%): Gelayerte Falle, Hinterhalt, manipulierter Weg
----


=== Beispiel-Encounters


_Combat Encounter_ (30% chance today):
* 1d4 Bandits (CR 1/8 each)
* 1 Dire Wolf (CR 1)
* 2 Wyverns (CR 6)
* Random selection from region_creatures table

_Social Encounter_ (25%):
* Merchant caravan looking for guards (Quest offer)
* Fleeing refugees (quest hook + food scarcity)
* Traveling preacher (info-gathering, spell casting service?)
* NPC needing rescue

_Discovery Encounter_ (15%):
* Abandoned Shrine (roleplay opportunity, potential loot)
* Wizard's Tower (dungeon? trap? treasure!)
* Dragon Lairs entrance (scouting opportunity)
* Ancient Bridge (beautiful/dangerous crossing)

'''


== Provisioning System



=== Provision Consumption


_Per Character per Day_: 1 Ration (Food + Water bundled)

* _Standard Ration_: 0.5 GP (dry rations, bread+water)
* _Quality Ration_: 2 GP (fresh meat, vegetables, wine)
* _Trail Ration_: 0.1 GP (minimal, low nutrition)


=== Where to get Provisions


. _Buy at Settlements_: Market prices variable
. _Forage (DC 12 Survival)_: 1d4 rations per day if successful
. **Hunt (1-2 hour, requires game): 2d6 rations if kills large animal
. **Fish (DC 10 if water nearby): 1d6 rations per hour


=== No Provisions = Exhaustion


----
Short Rest without Food:
  CON Save DC 10
  Fail: 1 Exhaustion gained

Long Rest without Food:
  CON Save DC 12
  Fail: 1 Exhaustion gained

2+ Exhaustion: Disadvantage on ALL Checks
3+ Exhaustion: Speed halved, Disadvantage on Attacks
4+ Exhaustion: Speed 0 (cannot move)
5+ Exhaustion: Hit Point maximum halved
6 Exhaustion: Death
----

'''


== Weather System (Monthly Calibration)



=== Base Weather per Region & Month


Nicht: "Winter ist immer schnee"
Ja: Schnee ist in Januar wahrscheinlich, in März möglich, in Juli unmöglich

_Beispiel: Temperate Region_


[cols="1,1,1,1",options="header"]
|===
| Month | Likely | Possible | Unlikely

| January | Snow, Cold | Rain, Fog | Heat, Drought
| April | Rain, Warm | Heat, Cold | Snow, Extreme
| July | Heat, Dry | Rain, Storms | Snow, Cold
| October | Rain, Cool | Heat, Wind | Snow (yet)
|===



=== Weather-Conditions


----
Weather Conditions (draw daily):
├── Clear (0% effect)
├── Cloudy (0% effect)
├── Light Rain (-5% travel speed)
├── Heavy Rain (-15% travel, DC 12 Survival)
├── Thunderstorm (-25% travel, DC 15 Survival, 5% Lightning Strike)
├── Fog (-20% Perception, -10% travel)
├── Heat Wave (travel -10%, Survival DC 12, need extra water)
├── Blizzard (travel -50%, Survival DC 15, Cold Damage 1d4/hour unprotected)
├── Sandstorm (travel -40%, Sand in Eyes -2 Perception)
└── Hurricane (impassable, stay in shelter)
----


=== Monthly Roll


----
GM each morning:
  Roll 1d100 on region_weather_profiles[harptos_month]

  0-40%:   Clear
  41-65%:  Cloudy
  66-80%:  Light Rain
  81-92%:  Heavy Rain
  93-98%:  Thunderstorm
  99-100%: Extreme (Blizzard in Winter, Sandstorm in Desert)

If result is "impossible" for season:
  Re-roll or GM override

  January in Temperate: Can't roll Heat → treat as next option
----

'''

=== Party Discovery Sharing


When one PC discovers location:
* _All present_ in that location discover it
* Party doesn't automatically share knowledge
* PCs must communicate between sessions ("Hey, I found the hidden temple")
* Mobile App: Party messaging for knowledge-sharing

== Time Tracking during Travel



=== Travel-Phases


Each day = 3 phases:

----
Morning (8am-12pm):
  - 1 travel phase (3-4 hours)
  - Breakfast time included

Afternoon (1pm-5pm):
  - 1 travel phase (4 hours)
  - Lunch/rest included

Evening (6pm-11pm):
  - Camp setup, dinner, rest
  - Possible campfire encounters
----


=== Long Rest during Travel


----
Party needs 8 hours uninterrupted sleep
Camping:
  - Setup: 30 min
  - Sleep: 8 hours
  - Breaking camp: 30 min
  - Total: 9 hours → loses 1 travel phase

Safe Town:
  - Inn: Full rest, no worry
  - Random House: Need persuasion (DC 12)
  - Wilderness: Risk of encounter during rest (50%)
----

'''


== Time-Skip Mechanic


GM can fast-forward time:


=== Mechanical Implications


----
Crafting Projects:
  - Continue NORMAL
  - Auto-rolled with standard DCs
  - Can fail during skip (GM determines)

Dragon Care:
  - Runs with ×0.25 modifier
  - Spürbar aber nicht voller Nachteil
  - Missing care → Bond loses BPs

Provisions:
  - Consumed per day as normal
  - Shortage warning if < 50% left

Weather:
  - Rolled per day as normal
  - Cumulative effects (3 days rain = wet camp)

Events:
  - Events in period trigger NACHTRÄGLICH am Ende
  - "3 weeks pass... you arrive at the city. Suddenly, town guards block entry..."

Item Durability:
  - NO natural decay over time
  - Only from active use
  - So no "rust prevention" mechanics

Item Maintenance:
  - If unused, stays perfect
  - If stored in damp place → GM discretion: potential rust
----

'''

├── destination_node_id (FK settlement)
├── distance_miles
├── terrain_type
