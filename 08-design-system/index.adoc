== Design Tokens


Design Tokens definieren die visuelle Sprache des Systems: Typographie, Farbsystem, Abstände, Schatten und weitere Designeigenschaften.


=== Typographie



[cols="1,1,1",options="header"]
|===
| Verwendung | Font | Einsatz

| _Display_ | Cinzel, Cinzel Decorative | Fantasy-Headers, Titel, Überschriften
| _Body_ | EB Garamond | Lesetext, Lore, Beschreibungen
| _Code/Stats_ | JetBrains Mono | Werte, Modifier, Datenbankreferenzen, Technische Infos
|===



==== Typography Scale


----
Display 1: 48px / 1.2 line-height
Display 2: 36px / 1.3 line-height
Heading 1: 28px / 1.4 line-height
Heading 2: 24px / 1.4 line-height
Body Large: 18px / 1.6 line-height
Body Regular: 16px / 1.6 line-height
Body Small: 14px / 1.5 line-height
Code: 13px / 1.5 line-height (Monospace)
----

== Komponenten-Architektur


Komponenten werden in einer Hierarchie organisiert: _Atoms → Molecules → Organisms → Templates_.


=== Atoms


Kleinste, unteilbare UI-Elemente. Keine Dependencies zu Business-Logic.


[cols="1,1,1",options="header"]
|===
| Komponente | Beschreibung | Props

| _StatBox_ | Ability Score: Modifier (groß), Score (klein), aufklappbar | value, modifier, label, color
| _PipTracker_ | Spell Slots, Hit Dice — ●●○ Pattern | max, current, type, color
| _Badge_ | Condition, Org, Renown, Rarity | text, variant (condition/org/rarity), color
| _ProgressBar_ | HP, XP, Durability, Care Score | current, max, type, color, showLabel
| _RollButton_ | Click-to-Roll mit Animation | onRoll, type (d4/d6/d20), animated
| _RestVotePopup_ | Pop-up für Rast-Abstimmung | options, onVote, players
| _HitDicePicker_ | Hit Dice pro Klasse auswählen + würfeln | classResources, onRoll, multiclass
| _ClassResourcePip_ | Wie SpellSlot-Pips, für Ki/Rage/etc. | max, current, type, color
| _AttunementSlots_ | 3 Slots Anzeige mit Item-Icons | items, maxSlots
| _InspirationToggle_ | Ein/Aus Badge | active, onToggle
| _NoteEditor_ | Freitext + Entity-Tags + Tag-Picker | content, onSave, entityTags
| _EntityMention_ | Inline @-Tag mit Auto-Complete | entityType, onSelect, suggestions
| _MapPolygonOverlay_ | Leaflet Polygon mit Hover/Click | polygon, onClick, onHover
| _MapTokenMarker_ | Draggable Character-Token auf Leaflet | token, onDrag, isDraggable
| _ImageWithBlurHash_ | BlurHash Placeholder → Crossfade → Real Image | src, blurHash, alt
|===



=== Molecules


Kombinationen von Atoms mit einfacher Logik und Interaktion.


[cols="1,1,1",options="header"]
|===
| Komponente | Beschreibung | Verwendung

| _TraitSlider_ | -5/+5 Slider, Farb-Gradient, Pol-Labels, Blender-Indikator | Trait-Bearbeitung, Character-Sheet
| _SpiderChart_ | D3-basiert, dynamisch, kontext-relevant, clickable Nodes | Ability-Scores, Attribute-Tracking
| _ItemCard_ | Icon, Rarity-Rahmen, Name, Stats-Preview | Inventory, Loot-Distribution
| _NPCDispositionCard_ | Portrait, Emoji, Name, bekannte Traits | NPC-Panel, Dialog-Vorbereitung
| _EquipmentSlot_ | Paper-Doll Slot, Rarity-Border, Click-to-Browse | Equipment-Grid, Paper-Doll
| _ModifierChain_ | Aufklappbar: Base + Prof + Trait + Item + Situational = Total | DC-Breakdown, Ability-Checks, Saves
| _RestVoteCard_ | Vote-State pro Spieler (✓/✗/waiting) | Rest-Voting Interface
| _EncounterSummary_ | Creatures, CR-Budget, Loot | Encounter-Builder
| _DialogueHistory_ | Nachricht-Liste mit Timestamps | Dialog-UI
| _DecisionSummary_ | Entscheidung mit Konsequenzen-Preview | Decision-Log
| _CraftingProgress_ | Project-Name, Steps, Timeline | Crafting-UI
| _OrganizationBadge_ | Org-Name, Rank, Reputation | Character-Sheet Org-Anzeige
|===



=== Organisms


Komplexe Komponenten mit mehreren Molecules und Business-Logic.


[cols="1,1,1",options="header"]
|===
| Komponente | Beschreibung | Verwendung

| _InventoryGrid_ | Filter, Sort, Container-Hierarchy (Backpack/Containers) | Character-Sheet Inventory
| _PaperDoll_ | BG3-Style, klassenspezifische Slots (Head/Armor/Hands/Feet) | Character-Sheet Equipment-Domain
| _DualSpiderChart_ | Reiter + Drache + Resonanz-Linien | Dragon-Bond Page
| _SessionPlanner_ | Graph-Editor mit Conditions | GM-Dashboard
| _SceneBar_ | Szenen-Info, Modus, Timer, NPCs | GM-Live-Steuerung
| _NPCManagementPanel_ | Profil + Disposition + Emotions | GM-Dashboard NPC-Section
| _MapViewer_ | Leaflet Canvas mit Tokens + Overlays | GM-Dashboard Map-Section
| _EncounterBuilder_ | Creature-Selector, CR-Budgeting, Loot-Gen | GM-Dashboard
| _PostSessionPipeline_ | 7-Schritte Workflow | GM-Dashboard nach Session
| _DialogueManager_ | Chat-Interface mit Skill-Checks | Social Mode
| _LootDistributionUI_ | Loot-Pool Anzeige, Draggable Zuweisung | Combat After-Action
| _RandomTableRoller_ | Table-Selector, Weighted Roll, Result | GM-Tools
| _CharacterOverview_ | Alle aktiven Sheets auf Blick (Mini-Übersicht) | GM-Dashboard
| _NotesPanel_ | Alle Spieler-Notizen, Filterable | GM-Dashboard
|===

== 13 Shared UI Patterns


Universelle UI-Muster, die über viele Systems hinweg wiederverwendet werden.


[cols="1,1,1,1",options="header"]
|===
| # | Pattern | Verwendungen | Beispiele

| 1 | _MechanicalEffect-Renderer_ | Universell auf 15+ Systemen | HP-Änderungen, Buff-Application, Damage-Rollup
| 2 | _ModifierChain_ | Aufklappbare Modifier-Kette | 10+ Use Cases (Saves, Checks, DC)
| 3 | _Progress/Score Meter_ | HP, XP, Durability, Care, etc. | 14+ Varianten mit unterschiedlichen Farben
| 4 | _Entity-Detail-Card_ | Universeller Container | NPC, Item, Creature, Quest, Organization
| 5 | _Spider Chart_ | D3-basiert | 3 Varianten (Standard, Dual, Context-filtered)
| 6 | _Filterable List/Grid_ | Überall (Inventory, Spells, Creatures) | 11+ Verwendungen
| 7 | _Visibility-System_ | GM-Toggle + Player-Filter | Alle Secrets, Loot, Hidden Info
| 8 | _Kontext-Modus System_ | 4 Modi × 9 Domains | Layout-Engine für Responsive-UI
| 9 | _Realtime-Events_ | Supabase Subscriptions | Live-Updates überall
| 10 | _Dice/Roll System_ | Würfel-UI + Animation | Button bis Result-Display
| 11 | _Equipment Slot System_ | Paper-Doll + Dragon Equipment | Rüstung, Schmuck, Dragon-Gear
| 12 | _Entity-Reference-Picker_ | Universeller Entity-Linker | Notes, Quick-Mode, Decisions
| 13 | _Graph/Node Editor Primitives_ | D3 Canvas | Session-Planer, Quest-Chains, Weltkarte
|===

== FSD-Struktur


Feature-Sliced Design (FSD) organisiert den Code nach Business-Features, nicht nach technischen Layern.


=== Verzeichnisstruktur


----
src/
├── app/                          # Application Layer
│   ├── routing/                  # Route-Definitionen
│   ├── providers/                # Context Providers, Zustand Stores
│   ├── global-layout/            # Root Layout
│   └── styles/                   # Global Styles, Design Tokens
│
├── pages/                        # Seiten / Route-Handler
│   ├── player/                   # Spieler-Seiten
│   │   ├── character-sheet/
│   │   ├── journal/
│   │   └── dragon-care/
│   ├── gm/                       # GM-Seiten
│   │   ├── dashboard/
│   │   ├── session-planner/
│   │   ├── arc-graph/
│   │   └── templates/
│   ├── lore/                     # Lore/Wiki-Seiten
│   └── shared-pages/             # Übergreifende Seiten
│
├── widgets/                      # Feature-Komplexe (User-Facing Modules)
│   ├── character-sheet/          # 9 Domains (abilities, combat, spells, etc.)
│   ├── gm-dashboard/
│   ├── lore-wiki/
│   ├── notes/
│   └── encounter-builder/
│
├── features/                     # Geschäfts-Features (Logik)
│   ├── dice-roller/              # Würfel-Mechanik
│   ├── spell-casting/            # Zauberei
│   ├── trait-evolution/          # Trait-System
│   ├── dragon-care/              # Drachen-Pflege
│   ├── session-planner/          # Session-Planung
│   ├── scene-management/         # Szenen-Steuerung
│   ├── npc-interaction/          # NPC-Dialoge
│   ├── popup-system/             # Popups
│   ├── level-up/                 # Level-Up-Dialog
│   ├── notes/                    # Notizen-System
│   ├── encounter-builder/        # Encounter-Konstruktion
│   ├── trade-system/             # Handels-Mechanik
│   ├── foundry-sync/             # Foundry Integration
│   ├── equipment-management/     # Equipment-System
│   └── crafting/                 # Crafting-Mechanik
│
├── entities/                     # Domain-Modelle (Zod Schemas)
│   ├── character/                # Character-Definitionen
│   ├── dragon/                   # Dragon-Definitionen
│   ├── npc/                      # NPC-Definitionen
│   ├── item/                     # Item-Definitionen
│   ├── spell/                    # Spell-Definitionen
│   ├── quest/                    # Quest-Definitionen
│   ├── organization/             # Organization-Definitionen
│   ├── creature/                 # Creature-Definitionen
│   ├── scene/                    # Scene-Definitionen
│   ├── campaign/                 # Campaign-Definitionen
│   ├── location/                 # Location-Definitionen
│   ├── rumor/                    # Rumor-Definitionen
│   ├── decision/                 # Decision-Definitionen
│   ├── session-plan/             # Session-Plan-Definitionen
│   ├── arc-graph/                # Arc-Graph-Definitionen
│   └── note/                     # Note-Definitionen
│
└── shared/                       # Geteilt (Keine Dependencies nach oben)
    ├── ui/
    │   ├── atoms/                # 14 Atoms
    │   ├── molecules/            # 17+ Molecules + Graph Primitives
    │   └── tokens/               # Design Tokens
    ├── api/                      # Supabase Client, REST Calls
    ├── hooks/                    # React Hooks (6+)
    │   ├── useCharacter/
    │   ├── useSupabase/
